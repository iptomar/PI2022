Triangle - Line

Build an algorithm that prints the triangle in the figure. The height must be entered by the user. Use the function: empty line(integer n)

        ler inteiro Height "height : "
        iterar inteiro i de 1 até Height + 1  passo 1
            executar line(i)
        fim iterar
    fim ProgramaPrincipal

    funcao vazio line(inteiro n)
