Problema Fluxograma Algorithmi
Build an algorithm that prints the figure on the console. Write a function called empty line20()

 
    inicio ProgramaPrincipal
        executar line()
        escrever "Numbers between 1 and 5\n"
        executar line()
        iterar inteiro i de 1 até 6  passo 1
            escrever i + "\n"

        executar line()

    funcao vazio line()
