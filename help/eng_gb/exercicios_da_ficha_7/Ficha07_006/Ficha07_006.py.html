Odd-Productory

Build a program that calculates the value of the formula. The value of n must be entered by the user.

Python 3

n =  int(input("Number of terms : "))
product = 1 
for i in range(1, n + 1, 1 ):
    f = 2 * i - 1 
    product = product * f
print( "Result = ", product  , end="")
