Iteration with reals [1.0 ... 2.0[

Problema Fluxograma Algorithmi
Write a program that iterates the numbers in the interval [1.0 , 2.0[ with a step of 0.1 NOTE: check the accuracy of the real numbers

 
    begin MainProgram
        for real i from 1.0 to 2.0  step 0.1
            write i + "\n"
        end for
    end MainProgram
