Perfect Number 

A perfect number is one in which the sum of its divisors is equal to itself

6 = 1 + 2 + 3 (perfect)
28 = 1 + 2 + 4 + 7 + 14 (perfect)

Build an algorithm that asks you to enter a number and then tells you whether it is "Perfect" or normal.


Input Output
28 Number : 28 Perfect
6 Number : 6 Perfect
5 Number : 5 Normal
37 Number : 37 Normal
12 Number : 12 Normal
30 Number : 30 Normal
29 Number : 29 Normal
22 Number : 22 Normal
50 Number : 50 Normal
7 Number : 7 Normal
