Average and Staff Note 

The Algorithms course has three evaluation tests with grades in the range [0.0, 100.0].
The final grade is calculated by the average of the two best graded tests.
Contruct an algorithm that:
- prompts all three for the score of the tests 
- Print the final score
- Print the staff grade - an integer in the range [0, 20]

NOTE:
The program must request the grades until a valid value is entered
