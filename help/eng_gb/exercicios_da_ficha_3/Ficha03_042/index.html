Cost of Car Travel 

Build a program to calculate the cost of a trip by an automobile.

The program should ask the user for:
- the initial kilometers
- the final kilometers.

The program should randomly generate :
- the fuel price (€/l) in the interval [1.0, 1.5[.
- the average consumption of the car (l/100km) in the interval [3.1 , 9.9[.

The program should print the cost of the trip in Euros and Cents.
