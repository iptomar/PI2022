Channel Audience 

A survey was conducted about the audience of RTP channels (RTP1, RTP2 and RTP3). 
Households that were not watching television or that were watching other channels are not included in the survey.

For each valid household surveyed, the number of the channel that was on the television was given.

a) reads the values of the channel (1,2,3) that people were watching
b) the sequence of channels ends with the value zero
c) calculate and display the number of viewers for each channel
d) Calculate and display the audience percentage for each channel
