Fibonacci sequence

Build an algorithm that prints the Fibonacci sequence numbers that are less than 100 on the console. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fibonacci_number

Python 3

# Programmer           Ant0ni0 M@ns0 - Ant0ni0 M@ns0


f0 = 1 
f1 = 1 
print(f0 ,  "\n", f1)
while f0 + f1 < 100  :
    fn = f0 + f1 
    print(fn)
    f1 = f0
    f0 = fn
