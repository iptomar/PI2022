Problema Fluxograma Algorithmi
Build an algorithm that prints the natural numbers with 1 digit in descending order on the console.

 
    begin MainProgram
        define integer i = 9
        while i >= 0 do
            write i + " "
            execute i = i - 1
        end while
    end MainProgram
Input Output
9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2 1 0
