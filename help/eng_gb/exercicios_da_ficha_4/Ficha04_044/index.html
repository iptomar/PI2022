Roots of a 2nd degree equation - II 

Build a program that calculates the value of the roots of a 2nd degree equation.

An equation has:

0 Roots - if delta is less than ZERO

1 Root - if delta is equal to ZERO 
          the root is given by the formula -b / 2a  

2 Roots - otherwise
          the roots are given by Bhaskara's formula 

NOTE:
Use a precision of 5 DECIMATE PARTS
