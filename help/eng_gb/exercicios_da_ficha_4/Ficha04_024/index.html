Pass, Fail or Excluded 

To pass a subject a student must have a positive average (>= 9.5) and attend at least 75% of the lessons of the 30 lessons taught.

If a student has not attended the minimum number of classes he is EXCLUDED.

Build an algorithm that asks the user for:
- the student's average
- his number of absences 
and then print out the student's status:
- Excluded - if he did not attend enough classes
- Passed - if the average is positive
- Fail - if the average is negative.
