Pass or Fail

Problema Fluxograma Algorithmi
Build an algorithm that checks whether a student is passing or failing given his final grade. A student passes if his grade is greater than or equal to 10.

 
    inicio ProgramaPrincipal
        ler inteiro grade "Grade\t: "
        se grade >= 10 então
            escrever "Approved"
        senão
            escrever "Reproved"
        fim se
    fim ProgramaPrincipal
Input Output
14 Grade : 14 Approved
4 Grade : 4 Reproved
20 Grade : 20 Approved
11 Grade : 11 Approved
16 Grade : 16 Approved
6 Grade : 6 Reproved
5 Grade : 5 Reproved
7 Grade : 7 Reproved
17 Grade : 17 Approved
13 Grade : 13 Approved
