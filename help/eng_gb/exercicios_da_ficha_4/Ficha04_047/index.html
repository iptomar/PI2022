Points and Lines 

Build a program that prompts the user:
- The equation of a line:
   aX + bY + c = 0
- The coordinates of a point
   x0, y0

and then tell whether the point is on the line or not.

Consider that a point lies on a line if the distance between the point and the line is less than 1.0E-6
