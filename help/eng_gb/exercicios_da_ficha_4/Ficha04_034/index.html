It can be a triangle 

Build an algorithm that asks the user for the three lengths and then checks whether those lengths can be the sides of a triangle.

Three lengths can be the sides of a triangle if the sum of any two is less than the other.

Example 1 :
6 - 10 - 2
It cannot be a triangle because 10 > 6+2

Example 2 :
6 - 8 - 10
Can be a triangle because:
  6 < 8 + 10
  8 < 6 + 10
  10 < 8 + 6
