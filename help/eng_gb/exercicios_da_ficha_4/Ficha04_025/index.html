Numbers in English 

Write an agorithm that prompts the user for an integer and then prints its name in English if it is between one and five or "Unknown" otherwise.


Input Output
4 Nnumber : 4 Four
8 Nnumber : 8 Unknown
0 Nnumber : 0 Unknown
1 Nnumber : 1 One
3 Nnumber : 3 Three
9 Nnumber : 9 Unknown
-2 Nnumber : -2 Unknown
5 Nnumber : 5 Five
-3 Nnumber : -3 Unknown
2 Nnumber : 2 Two
