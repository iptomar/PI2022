Circumferences and Lines 

Build a program that prompts the user:
- The equation of a line:
   aX + bY + c = 0
- The position of the circle
   - The coordinates of the center C(x0, y0)
   - The value of the radius (r) 

and then state whether the line is tangent, secant, or exterior

Consider rounding errors of 1.0E-6
