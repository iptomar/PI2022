Body Mass Index

BMI (body mass index) is given by the ratio between a person's weight (kilograms) and height squared. Build a program that asks the user for his weight and height, and then print out his BMI score: BMI Rating < 18.5 Low [18.5 , 25.0[ Normal [25.0 , 30.0[ Fat [30.0 - 35.0[ Obese > 35.0 Extremely obese
Fluxograma Algorithmi 
    inicio ProgramaPrincipal
        ler real x "Weight (kg) \t: "
        ler real y "Height (m) \t: "
        definir real imc = x / (y ^ 2)
        escrever "IMC \t\t= " + imc
        escrever "\nClassification \t= "
        se imc < 18.5 então
            escrever "Thin"
        senão
            se imc < 25 então
                escrever "Normal"
            senão
                se imc < 30 então
                    escrever "Fat"
                senão
                    se imc < 35 então
                        escrever "obese"
                    senão
                        escrever "Extremely obese"
                    fim se
                fim se
            fim se
        fim se
    fim ProgramaPrincipal
