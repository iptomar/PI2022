Expressions

Computational expressions

Expressions are a set of variables, constants, operators, and functions. Expressions are evaluated from left to right, respecting the priority of operators, and curved parentheses change that priority.

Numerical expressions

The result of a numeric expression is always an integer if all operands are integers and real if at least one of them is real.
